Industrial Training Modules

ITM course serves as a hot melting pot of the dynamically changing needs of the industry. These modules are designed in collaboration with top industry and academic experts across various fields. A short term ITM is typically held for 2-3 days, whereas a full course in ITM requires 5-7 days depending upon the nature of training required.

Content and general structure of an ITM

Circuit Branches:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Machine learning
  • Semiconductor Technology (material selection, fabrication, designing and packaging)
  • Semiconductors in emerging technical drive through
  • Data mining and analytics (Scope and Impact)

Non-Circuit Branches:

  • Automobile Design
  • Strength of Materials
  • Mechanical failures and their analysis

Inter-Disciplinary:

  • Nano science an emerging field
  • Simulation in various branches of engineering and science
  • Theoretical study approach in various fields related to science and engineering

The entire training module is structured to reflect an extra ordinary show of theoretical understanding, live and practical demonstration of the concepts and honing the industrially relevant skillsets of the participants.

Opening Session

Module 01

Theoretical module, covering the entire evolution of the relevant technology to the present-day state of the art technology.

Panel will consist of -

Industry expert
Academic expert
Lab technician

Time duration:

2-3 hours

Mid sessions

Module 02/03

These modules are practical demonstration and hands on/tool training based. After the required training, Students will be given individual or group projects to work on. Performance in the project will help the TOSE team to set a learning marker of the student and eventually in the final process of certification

Support team will consist of -

Professor/Research Scholar
Industry expert
Technicians

Time duration:

4-6 hours

Penultimate Session

Module 03/04

This session will have exciting competitions among the participants. The nature of the competition is self-leveraging and help the participants develop sharp acumen in deep technical crisis.

Organizers will consist of -

Research Scholar
2-3 students Volunteer
Two/three technicians

Time duration:

1-2 hours

Concluding Session

Module 05

This will be the concluding session. Summarizing the entire learning along with the distribution of certificates and awards towards the end of the program.

Organizing team -

Chief Guest/Patron
Scientists/Professors
Industry experts
2-3 students Volunteer
Two/three technicians

Time duration:

1 hours
