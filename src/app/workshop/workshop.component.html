Plug n Play Workshop in Schools

There are various interesting topics in science and mathematics which need an “Out of the Book” treatment. Under a typical classroom session with a prescribed format of syllabus and set examination pattern, it becomes nearly impossible on the educator’s end to bring the true joy of learning these concepts. TOSE acknowledges the limitations of system and is dedicated to workout many exciting and enjoyable learning package solution at school level.

Plug n Play (PnP) workshops contents and general structure:

Mathematics:

  • Understanding number theory and system
  • Basic geometry and related theorems
  • Nature of algebraic equation and their Handling

Physics:

  • Mechanics elaborate session (theory+numericals+practical models etc.)
  • Understanding nature of Gravity (freefall, blackhole)
  • Heat and Temperature
  • Mathematical methods in Physics

Chemistry:

  • World of Chemical reactions
  • World of organic chemistry

Biology:

  • World of Cell biology
  • Evolution theory or a fact

Miscellaneous:

  • Technology impacting human lives
